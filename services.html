Our Services

Prime Brokerage & Financing Optimisation

Comprehensive review and optimization of Prime Brokerage relationships, synthetic financing rates, and borrow costs. We analyze counterparty allocations, haircut schedules, and cross-margining opportunities to lower execution drag and boost fund returns.

Repo & Securities Finance

Strategic advisory for repo, reverse repo, and cash reinvestment structures. We help clients maximize yield on unencumbered balances and reduce secured funding costs across global counterparties.

Margin Replication & Analytics

Independent replication and transparency for initial and variation margin calculations. We identify overcharging, policy discrepancies, and cost drivers across OTC derivatives and cleared products.

Capital & Cross-Asset Optimisation

Quantitative strategies to minimize aggregate margin requirements, optimize cross-asset collateral allocation, and improve overall performance ratios.

Treasury & Liquidity Management

Integrated cash flow reporting and liquidity analytics designed to optimize cash drag, track intraday buffer requirements, and ensure seamless collateral availability.
